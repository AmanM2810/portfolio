About Me

Hi, I’m Anya Gupta — a character designer and illustrator with a love for visual storytelling, whimsical worlds, and emotionally rich characters. My work blends soft palettes, expressive forms, and narrative-driven compositions to bring personalities and places to life.

Whether it's designing a cozy room, a quiet ramen shop, or a curious cat’s adventure, I focus on creating illustrations that make people pause, feel, and imagine. Inspired by everyday moments, film stills, and childhood nostalgia — I’m here to turn simple ideas into memorable scenes.
